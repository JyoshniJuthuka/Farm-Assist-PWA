📘 Govt. Schemes & Community

  • Income
    PM-Kisan Yojana
    ₹6,000/year in 3 installments for eligible farmers
    Learn More

    The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan) provides income support of ₹6,000 per year, paid in three equal installments. Registration can be done via the PM-Kisan portal or Common Service Centers.

  • Insurance
    Crop Insurance
    Protects farmers from crop loss due to natural calamities
    Learn More

    Crop Insurance provides coverage against crop loss due to floods, droughts, pests, and diseases. Premium subsidies are available for eligible farmers.

  • Soil
    Soil Health Card
    Helps farmers understand nutrient status of their soil
    Learn More

    Soil Health Card scheme provides farmers with detailed information on the nutrient status of their soil and recommendations for improving fertility.
