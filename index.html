What is PM-Kisan and its benefits?

The **Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan)** scheme provides income support of **₹6,000 per year**, paid in **three equal instalments of ₹2,000 each**. Eligible farmers with cultivable land holdings can register via the PM-Kisan Portal or through Common Service Centers.
